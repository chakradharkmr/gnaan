About Gnaan

Gnaan Foundation is a registered non profit organisation founded by a group of IT professionals, aimed at giving the gift of knowledge where it really matters : at the individual level. The organization is focused at providing education in any form to those who are deprived of it. Personal tutoring, donation of books, provision of basic infrastructural requirements that are crucial for the childâ€™s educational enrichment ; all are a priority of Gnaan.

Volunteering open

According to Govt statistics, there is a shortage of more than 1 million teachers in Primary schools nationwide. We also hope to compensate for that gap by whatever means we can. We believe that change can be achieved by empowering the budding minds of tomorrow. Empowering them so they can realize their dreams.

Why volunteer at Gnaan

We believe that an hour spent educating a group of children personally is more likely to make a difference than support extended at the administration level.
